Notre Histoire — Fondateur

We Built the Company
We Wished Existed.

International vehicle shipping was broken. Hidden fees, no real tracking, unreachable support — clients were losing sleep over vehicles worth tens of thousands of dollars with no idea where they were.

Makaship was founded in Montréal to change that. We built a process around three things: photo documentation at every stage, real-time tracking from loading to delivery, and a direct line to a real person who knows your shipment.

Today, we ship vehicles and cargo to over 50 destinations worldwide — and every single shipment gets the same level of care as the first one.

"We treat every client's vehicle like it's our own. That's not a tagline — it's how we operate every single day."
— The Makaship Team
